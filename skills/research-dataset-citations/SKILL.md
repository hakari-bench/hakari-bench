# Research Dataset Citations

The canonical citation metadata workflow lives in
[`docs/dataset_citation_metadata.md`](../../docs/dataset_citation_metadata.md).

Before adding, auditing, or repairing dataset references, read that document and
follow it as the source of truth for:

- source priority,
- paper versus non-paper classification,
- `source_confidence` labels,
- dataset-level and task-level metadata shape,
- BibTeX and citation key policy,
- validation expectations.

Do not maintain citation workflow details in this skill file. Update
`docs/dataset_citation_metadata.md` instead.
