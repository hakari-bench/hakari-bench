## Overview

`NanoMTEB-Misc / 2022_fa` is the Persian document split of the TREC 2022
NeuCLIR retrieval task. Persian information needs retrieve Persian news
articles from a hard-negative candidate pool.

## Details

### What the Original Data Measures

[Overview of the TREC 2022 NeuCLIR Track](https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.12367)
describes NeuCLIR as an ad hoc cross-language retrieval benchmark over Chinese,
Persian, and Russian newswire documents. The original CLIR setting used English
topics to retrieve non-English news articles; this Nano split uses the Persian
topic text and Persian documents, so it tests monolingual Persian news retrieval
within the NeuCLIR topic and judgment structure.

The NeuCLIR paper reports that documents came from Common Crawl news pages,
were language-filtered, and were judged with graded TREC relevance labels. This
matters here because many topics have many relevant articles, and a single exact
headline match is not the only valid target.

### Observed Data Profile

The Nano split has 45 queries, 8,882 documents, and 1,131 positive qrel rows.
Queries average 83.13 characters and documents average 2,818.76 characters.
The positive distribution is broad: the average is 25.13 positives per query,
with a maximum of 100. Sampled documents are Persian news articles with
headlines and long article bodies.

### BM25 Difficulty

Using the dataset-provided BM25 candidate column, BM25 reaches nDCG@10 =
0.2890 and hit@10 = 0.5778. It ranks 12 queries at rank 1 and 26 queries within
the top 10; every query has at least one positive in the top 100.

Sparse retrieval finds articles with distinctive repeated topic terms, but the
large multi-positive pools and long news articles make top-10 ordering hard.
The missed examples show BM25 retrieving same-topic or adjacent news before one
of the judged positives.

### Training Data That May Help

Useful training data includes Persian news retrieval, NeuCLIR-style translated
topic-document pairs, multilingual CLIR corpora, and hard negatives from same
event, same entity, or same news category articles.

### Synthetic Data Guidance

Generate Persian information needs from non-evaluation Persian news articles
and keep multiple relevant articles for broad topics. Include hard negatives
that share the key named entities but answer a different event angle. Avoid
single-answer QA synthesis; this task rewards ranked retrieval over many
relevant news documents.
