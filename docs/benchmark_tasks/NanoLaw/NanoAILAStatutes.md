## Overview

`NanoAILAStatutes` is an English legal statute-retrieval task. Each query is a
long Indian legal fact pattern, and the retriever must find statutory provisions
that are applicable to the situation.

## Details

### What the Original Data Measures

[Overview of the FIRE 2019 AILA Track: Artificial Intelligence for Legal Assistance](https://ceur-ws.org/Vol-2517/T1-1.pdf)
states that AILA 2019 included a statute retrieval task in which systems were
given 50 factual scenarios and asked to identify the most relevant statutes. The
paper reports that the statute pool was built from frequently cited Indian legal
sections, reduced to 197 statutes after removing repealed entries, and that
statute text includes titles and descriptions.

The [MTEB AILA_statutes dataset card](https://huggingface.co/datasets/mteb/AILA_statutes)
describes the task as identifying the most relevant statutes for a given
situation, using the same AILA 2019 release.

### Observed Data Profile

The Nano split has 50 queries, 82 statute documents, and 217 positive qrels.
Queries are the same long factual scenarios as the precedent task and average
3,038.42 characters. Statute documents are much shorter than case judgments,
averaging 1,972.63 characters, and consist of statute titles plus descriptive
legal text.

Every query has multiple positive statutes. The average is 4.34 positives per
query, with a median of 4.5 and maximum of 5.

### BM25 Difficulty

Using the dataset-provided BM25 candidate column, BM25 reaches nDCG@10 =
0.1900 and hit@10 = 0.6600. It ranks a positive first for 6 queries. The task is
hard for lexical retrieval because long factual narratives may imply statutory
requirements without repeating statute titles or exact wording.

### Training Data That May Help

Helpful training data includes fact-to-statute retrieval, legal entailment over
statutory provisions, and hard negatives from neighboring criminal procedure,
evidence, constitutional, and penal-code sections. Training should retain the
multi-positive nature because several provisions can govern one scenario.

### Synthetic Data Guidance

Generate case-like factual narratives with legal issues that map to several
statutory provisions. Positives should be actual or realistic statute sections;
hard negatives should mention related legal concepts but not apply to the
scenario.
