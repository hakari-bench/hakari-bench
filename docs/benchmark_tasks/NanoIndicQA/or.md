## Overview

`NanoIndicQA / or` is the Odia split of IndicQA retrieval. Odia questions
retrieve Odia context paragraphs from a compact corpus.

## Details

### What the Original Data Measures

[Towards Leaving No Indic Language Behind](https://arxiv.org/abs/2212.05409)
describes IndicQA as a cloze-style reading-comprehension benchmark within
IndicXTREME. The retrieval version measures question-to-context matching.

### Observed Data Profile

The Nano split has 200 queries, 252 documents, and 201 positive qrel rows. One
query has two positives. Queries average 57.16 characters and documents average
801.92 characters. The first examples are all tied to an RSS/Indian independence
movement context.

### BM25 Difficulty

Using the dataset-provided BM25 candidate column, BM25 reaches nDCG@10 = 0.6041
and hit@10 = 0.7250. It ranks 98 positives at rank 1 and 145 in the top 10. All
positives are within the top 100.

### Training Data That May Help

Odia QA retrieval, Odia Wikipedia passages, and multilingual IndicQA-style data
are useful. Hard negatives should use related organizations, movements, and
history topics.

### Synthetic Data Guidance

Generate Odia questions from paragraphs and preserve context paragraphs as
positives. Include multiple questions per context and hard negatives with shared
entities or political terms.
