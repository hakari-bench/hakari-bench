## Overview

FEVER is claim evidence retrieval over Wikipedia-style documents.
`NanoBEIR-th__NanoFEVER` uses Thai translated claims and evidence passages.

## Details

### What the Original Data Measures

[FEVER](https://arxiv.org/abs/1803.05355) evaluates fact verification with
retrieved evidence. BEIR uses the retrieval component, and MMTEB provides
multilingual context.

### Observed Data Profile

The task has 50 queries, 4,996 documents, and 57 positive qrels. Most queries
have one positive. Queries average 46.88 characters, and documents average
1,084.74 characters.

### BM25 Difficulty

BM25 reaches nDCG@10 = 0.7001 and hit@10 = 0.8200. Median first-positive rank is
1.0, so many claims preserve strong entity cues.

### Training Data That May Help

Use Thai claim-evidence retrieval, Wikipedia evidence mining, and multilingual
fact-checking, excluding FEVER, BEIR, NanoBEIR, and translated overlaps.

### Synthetic Data Guidance

Generate Thai factual claims from encyclopedia passages with hard negatives from
related entities or events.
