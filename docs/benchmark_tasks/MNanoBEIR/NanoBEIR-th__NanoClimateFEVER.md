## Overview

Climate-FEVER is climate claim evidence retrieval. `NanoBEIR-th__NanoClimateFEVER`
uses Thai translated claims to retrieve Thai translated evidence passages.

## Details

### What the Original Data Measures

[CLIMATE-FEVER](https://arxiv.org/abs/2012.00614) evaluates whether systems can
retrieve evidence for real-world climate claims. BEIR includes it as
claim-evidence retrieval, and MMTEB provides multilingual context.

### Observed Data Profile

The task has 50 queries, 3,408 documents, and 148 positive qrels. Queries are
mostly multi-positive, averaging 2.96 positives. Thai queries average 118.64
characters, and documents average 1,395.41 characters.

### BM25 Difficulty

BM25 reaches nDCG@10 = 0.2368 and hit@10 = 0.5400, with median first-positive
rank 7.0. Lexical overlap is not enough for robust Thai evidence ranking.

### Training Data That May Help

Use non-overlapping Thai or multilingual climate fact-checking, claim-evidence
retrieval, and scientific passage ranking. Exclude CLIMATE-FEVER, BEIR,
NanoBEIR, and translated evaluation records.

### Synthetic Data Guidance

Generate Thai climate claims from non-evaluation evidence passages. Hard
negatives should reuse climate terms without verifying the claim.
