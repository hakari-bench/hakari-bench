## Overview

Climate-FEVER is climate claim evidence retrieval. `NanoBEIR-vi__NanoClimateFEVER`
uses Vietnamese translated claims to retrieve Vietnamese translated evidence.

## Details

### What the Original Data Measures

[CLIMATE-FEVER](https://arxiv.org/abs/2012.00614) evaluates evidence retrieval
for real-world climate claims. BEIR includes it as claim-evidence retrieval, and
MMTEB supplies multilingual context.

### Observed Data Profile

The task has 50 queries, 3,408 documents, and 148 qrels. Queries average 2.96
positives, with 44 multi-positive queries. Query length averages 133.76
characters, and documents average 1,589.80 characters.

### BM25 Difficulty

BM25 reaches nDCG@10 = 0.2668 and hit@10 = 0.6000. Median first-positive rank is
5.5, so Vietnamese climate evidence retrieval requires more than term matching.

### Training Data That May Help

Use non-overlapping Vietnamese climate fact-checking, scientific evidence
retrieval, and multilingual claim-evidence data. Exclude CLIMATE-FEVER, BEIR,
NanoBEIR, and translated evaluation records.

### Synthetic Data Guidance

Generate Vietnamese climate claims from evidence passages and use hard negatives
with similar climate terms but no verifying evidence.
